What is Propaganda Playground?

Propaganda Playground is a critical design brand that aims to reveal serious issues of the power system to the public in an easy-to-understand way. Through gamification and interactive visual systems, it engages users and allows them to gradually experience firsthand how design shapes emotions, beliefs, and political power.
