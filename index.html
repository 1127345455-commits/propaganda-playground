Welcome to the Playground

Propaganda Playground is a critical design brand disguised as a playful space.

In here, bright colors feel friendly, soft shapes feel harmless, and cheerful characters invite you to explore.

But design is never neutral. Emotional influence is effective because it is not perceived as manipulation.

Through interactive visual systems and playful interfaces, Propaganda Playground reveals how visual design quietly shapes emotions, beliefs, and the way power is perceived.

Rather than explaining propaganda directly, this playground allows visitors to experience it —to feel how comfort, familiarity, and enjoyment can soften critical thinking.

It doesn’t explain propaganda. It lets you feel it.
